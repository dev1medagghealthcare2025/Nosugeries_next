# STARTUP JOURNEY OF MEDAGG! - medagg

![](https://medagghealthcare.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/1647365708371-300x197.jpeg)

We started off 3 years ago with “Medical Aggregation” in mind. All we knew was that this theme made sense given how fragmented the medical services industry was and still is.

The Consulting vertical proved to be the best conduit to bring potential aggregation ideas to fruition.

We branched off into Transaction Advisory seeing the need for fund raising amongst businesses who wish to consolidate/aggregate.

In the process we stumbled upon businesses in which we got so engrossed and involved that we made Strategic Investments ourselves! We are now proud to be part of the below portfolio.

But we have stayed our course in developing our own aggregation platform as well. **Medagg Healthcare** is emerging as the largest search engine platform for secondary hospitals in Tamil Nadu.
