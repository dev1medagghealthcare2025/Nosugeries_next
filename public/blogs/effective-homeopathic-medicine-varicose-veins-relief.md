Understanding Varicose Veins
----------------------------

![](https://images.surferseo.art/8161f9a6-a454-4c76-9771-c12ff71fe8d0.jpeg)

Varicose veins occur when vein valves weaken or are damaged, allowing blood to pool and distend the vein. This most often affects the lower limbs and tends to worsen with prolonged standing or sitting. The visible result can be blue or purple, bulging, twisted veins along with aching, heaviness, and leg swelling.

Complications and Related Conditions
------------------------------------

If untreated, varicose veins can lead to important complications:

- Bleeding from fragile, superficial veins after minor trauma
- Skin inflammation and discoloration, progressing to venous eczema (stasis dermatitis)
- Painful, slow-healing venous leg ulcers

Embracing Non-Surgical Solutions: Interventional Radiology and Endovenous Ablation
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Endovenous ablation is a modern, minimally invasive treatment guided by interventional radiology. Laser or radiofrequency energy is delivered inside the diseased vein via a thin catheter, heating the vein wall to seal it shut. Blood is rerouted to healthy veins, reducing pressure and relieving symptoms.

Benefits of Endovenous Ablation
-------------------------------

- Minimally invasive: tiny puncture, no large incisions
- Quick recovery: many patients resume normal activity in 1–2 days
- Effective results: high success rates for symptom improvement and appearance
- Lower risk than surgery and typically done with local anesthesia

Lifestyle Changes and Natural Therapies
---------------------------------------

![](https://images.surferseo.art/e73bf4a0-e349-4297-b9bd-d8070da9eb64.jpeg)

- Move regularly: walking, cycling, and swimming improve calf muscle pump and venous return
- Avoid prolonged sitting/standing: take movement breaks and calf raises
- Maintain a healthy weight to reduce venous pressure
- Elevate legs several times daily to reduce swelling
- Wear graduated compression stockings as advised by your clinician
